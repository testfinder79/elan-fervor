How to Use Élan Fervor

Make the most of your actives with this simple routine.

PM Routine (3–4 nights/week to start)

Cleanse → Apply a pea-size amount of your Granactive Retinoid treatment → Follow with Glowing Restoring Moisturiser. Retinoids support collagen and elastin; squalane + hyaluronic acid seal and boost hydration.

Why It Works

Peptides signal skin to improve firmness, while hyaluronic acid attracts moisture for a plump finish. Antioxidants like CoQ10, vitamin C, and vitamin E help defend against environmental stressors.

Ingredient Highlights

  • Granactive Retinoid — gentle yet effective vitamin-A technology.
  • Matrixyl Synthe’6® / Matrixyl® 3000 — peptide duo for bounce and smoothness.
  • Hyaluronic Acid — deep hydration for a plump, dewy look.
  • CoQ10, Vitamins C & E — antioxidant support.
  • Squalane & Aloe — comfort and moisture for all skin types.
