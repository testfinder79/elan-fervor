Our Story

High-performance skincare that treats skin with respect.

Why Élan Fervor

We craft formulas that balance real results with gentle care. Our approach pairs proven actives—retinoids, peptides, and hyaluronic acid—with soothing botanicals for skin that looks smoother, bouncier, and more radiant, without heavy feel or harshness.

Ingredient Philosophy

  • Granactive Retinoid to refine texture and tone with less irritation than traditional retinol.
  • Peptides (Matrixyl Synthe’6®, Matrixyl® 3000) to encourage collagen and firmness.
  • Hyaluronic Acid for deep hydration and a plump look.
  • CoQ10 + Vitamins C & E to provide antioxidant support.
  • Squalane & Aloe to moisturize, soften, and soothe all skin types.
  • Botanical Allies like chamomile, rosehip, sea buckthorn, geranium, and more to nourish and calm.
